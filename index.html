Are you intrested in the technical side of events? Why not come along to one of our New Members’ Introduction sessions, where we will go through some of our huge range of kit and how to use it. No experience is required - all knowledge levels are welcome. We will provide you with the training required to be part of our team, so come along and see what it’s all about. TEC is all about getting stuck in!

Once you’ve attended a New Members’ Introduction, you can come along to any TEC event. Coming along doesn't mean you're committing to TEC - why not come and try it out? We'd love to have you as part of the team.

About Us

TEC provides extensive training

On everything from setting up a small event to balanced audio or lighting design. As well as our in-house training, you can learn to drive our van and trailer, as well as attend First Aid and Fire Safety training provided by the Students’ Union.

Don't think we're only about hard work!

Running events may be what we do but we also enjoy kicking back with a range of social activities - whether it’s go karting, watching musicals or hanging out in the pub.
