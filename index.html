Get Involved with Something Unique!

By students, for students

TEC is a UoNSU Student Group providing technical services to the Union and other Groups, the University and external companies. We work on a range of events, from gigs at London's O2 to student back gardens.

You can be one of the volunteers making it continue year on year.
