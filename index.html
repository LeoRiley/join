About Us

TEC provides extensive training

There is training on everything, from setting up a small event to balanced audio or lighting design. As well as our in house training, you can learn to drive our van and trailer, as well as attend First Aid and Fire Safety training provided by the Students’ Union.

Don't think we're only about hard work!

Running events may be what we do but we also enjoy kicking back with a range of social activities - whether it’s go karting, watching musicals or hanging out in the pub.
