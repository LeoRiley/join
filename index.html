What is TEC?

TEC is a UoNSU Student Group providing technical services to the Union, Societies, the University and external clients. We work on a range of events, from gigs at London's O2 to student back gardens. We train on, maintain and operate our own lighting and sound equipment.
In TEC you can expect to take part in a wide range of activities of your choice, including:

  • Learning technical skills from other members during training
  • Setting up, operating and dismantling equipment at events
  • Communicating with clients and planning events
  • Preparing and maintaining equipment in our storeroom on King's Meadow Campus
  • Driving our van (requires additional training and 1 year of owning a driver's license)

You can be one of the volunteers making it continue year on year.

It's free and always will be.
